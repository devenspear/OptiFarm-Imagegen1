### **Optimist Farm — Writing Bible v2.4**

### **1\) Vision & Audience**

* **Brand promise:** Optimist Farm helps families grow everyday virtues through short, rhythmic, multimedia, interactive stories designed for tablets and large screens with calm taps and cozy soundscapes.  
* **Primary audience:** Children ages 3–5 (read-aloud with a grown-up).  
* **Secondary audience:** Caregivers who want one do-today ritual after each story.  
* **Digital must-haves baked into every story:**  
  * Narration toggle \+ soft SFX (windmill whoosh, pond ripple, bell chime).  
  * 1 tap moment per spread, a sound when reader taps (e.g., bees buzzing, pond ripple, door creaking, bell chiming).  
  * Pippin is revealed Visually in every scene on every page (seek & notice).  
  * Accessibility: captions toggle, large tap targets, high contrast.  
  * Saturated, rainbow-forward color palette for a bright, optimistic feel.  
* **Outcomes after one read:**  
  * Child repeats one mantra.  
  * Family tries one micro-ritual the same day.

### **5\) Story Engine (screen-paced & kid-simple)**

**Six-Beat Arc (\~20–24 screens):**

1. **Setup** — Friend \+ Place \+ Aim (2 short rhythmic lines).  
2. **Problem** — One wobble (sensory \+ onomatopoeia).  
3. **Mentor/Light** — Ollie (or peer) whispers a ≤18-word line; soft chime; tiny visual glow.  
4. **Practice/Growth** — Two mini-tries; one visible tweak each; gentle self-talk.  
5. **Climax/Win** — Small, vivid success (carrots pop; kite lifts; tower holds; calm approach works).  
6. Resolution \+ Final Affirmation — Meadow cheer or starry quiet \+ a question inviting action today.  
   Promise: Happy ending in each story.

**Digital cadence layered on those beats**

* **Tap cues (predictable):** ribbon/leaf/tail/can; 1–2 per act.  
* **Pippin:** Visually appears on every page: tap on Pippin to trigger a cheerful “Pippin smile” sound; optional subtle wiggle.  
* **Audio:** gentle SFX bed; mentor chime on Ollie’s line.  
* **Colors:** saturated, rainbow-forward palette throughout.

### **6\) Creator Toolkit**

#### **A) Signature Closers (pick one per story)**

* **Star Look-Up** — Everyone pauses, looks at the night sky.  
  * *Action cue:* “Let’s step outside and find one tiny star tonight.”  
* **Meadow Cheer & Share** — Friends cheer and share something small (seat, snack, tool).  
  * *Action cue:* “Share one thing at dinner—seat, napkin, or a smile.”  
* **Solo Quiet Pride → Friends Arrive** — Hero takes a proud breath; friends join for a soft high-five/hug.  
  * *Action cue:* “Put your hand on your heart and take one proud breath.”  
* *Always follow with the Final Affirmation question (one line) \+ on-screen button/tap prompt.*

#### **B) Digital-First Format Rules (primary platform)**

* Narration & SFX  
* 1–2 taps per act  
* Visual image of Pippin is hidden (but not in text narrative) on every page; tap \= smile sound  
* Saturated, rainbow colors  
* Captions toggle, large targets, high contrast

#### **C) Canon Beats & Lines (Reusable “lego bricks”)**

* **Mentor patterns (Ollie):**  
  * “Choose kindness; let grumps fade away.”  
  * “Each fall helps you grow—see what the pieces teach.”  
  * “Instead of ‘I can’t,’ try ‘I can try.’”  
  * “Quiet hearts make brave friends.”  
* **Mantras:**  
  * Thank you, Sun… Rain… Earth. • Try, tie, try again. • Choose kindness. • Quiet hearts make brave friends. • Wobbly can still be brave.

#### **D) Spread-by-Spread Writing Guide (expanded, simple language)**

1. **1–2 Setup (Friend \+ Place \+ Aim)**  
   * Two short lines. One sense word.  
   * *“Barnaby is in the garden. Today he will water and thank.”*  
2. **3–6 Problem (One wobble \+ sound word)**  
   * One clear problem, one sound word.  
   * *“Whoosh\! The wind tips the can. Water splatters the path.”*  
3. **7–10 Mentor / Light (≤18 words \+ chime)**  
   * One whisper-line from Ollie (or wise peer).  
   * *“Notice your helpers. Say thank you to one.” (chime)*  
4. **11–16 Practice / Growth (Two tries, one tweak each)**  
   * Try \#1: small change. Try \#2: add a friend/tool/breath.  
   * *“Barnaby holds the can low. Then asks Pearl to steady it.”*  
5. **16–18 Climax / Win (show it clearly)**  
   * One image, one sentence.  
   * *“The water lands just right. The carrots pop with green.”*  
6. **18–24 Resolution \+ Final Affirmation (choose a closer)**  
   * Closer type (Star / Meadow Share / Solo Pride).  
   * One Final Affirmation question.  
   * *“What will you try today?”*  
   * (Pippin Visually still appears; tap for smile sound.)

#### **E) Back-Matter Pack (Digital)**

* **For Grown-ups (1 screen):** Why this virtue matters at 3–5 \+ two Try-Tonight tips.  
* **For Kids (1–2 screens):** 3 open questions \+ Little Sprouts mini-activity \+ Find Pippin recap.  
* **Bonus:** “Tap to hear Ollie say the mantra.”

#### **F) Virtue → Setting → Prop Matrix (quick ideation)**

* Gratitude × Garden × Watering can (Barnaby)  
* Perseverance × Windmill/Meadow × Ribbon/Kite (Pearl or Lil Dax)  
* Kindness/Sharing × Meadow × Blanket/Clover (Christy, Clementine)  
* Respect/Trust × Meadow Edge × Quiet blanket/hand-on-heart (Dugan)  
* Resilience × Barn × Blocks/Level (Gus/Posie)  
* Responsibility × Barn Pegs × Tool (Delilah coaching; Lil Dax helps)  
* Patience × Seed Trays × Sun patch (Lillie \+ Barnaby)

#### **G) Micro-Style Rules (Keep Your Sound)**

* 8–12 syllables per line; short sentences.  
* Concrete verbs; one sensory image per screen.  
* Mentor lines ≤18 words; whisper-able & quotable.  
* Onomatopoeia only in Problem/Growth beats.  
* Happy ending in each story.
